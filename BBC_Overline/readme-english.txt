[color=purple][b][size=10pt]Compatibility[/size][/b][/color]
SMF 2.0.x


[color=purple][b][size=10pt]Description[/size][/b][/color] 
The Overline BBCode modification adds a BBCode button next to the "Strikethrough" button on the post page. You can use the [nobbc][o][/nobbc] BBCode to place a line above the selected text, as well as/as opposed to just Strike Through or Underline.


[color=purple][b][size=10pt]Supported Languages[/size][/b][/color] 
English, German, European Portuguese, Brazilian Portuguese
